About

My Story

Hi, I’m Ashley Fischer! I was born and raised in Indiana (Go Hoosiers!), new to Kansas City after living in both Los Angeles and Austin, TX. I’m a Film/TV editor turned Graphic Designer and User Interface Designer. I’m currently finishing my UI Design Certification and Front-End Development Specialization through CareerFoundry.

My friends often describe be as a “go-getter” due to my motivation to constantly learn and work on creative projects. I consider myself a forever student,” because like design trends, things are constantly changing, updating, and evolving. I’m eager to keep learning and to stay in tune with the latest digital design and technology trends through continued coursework and professional development.

My background in the TV & Film industry and graphic design has given me valuable experience in team collaboration, time-management, and remote work communication. I can offer my creative flair and knowledge in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign and Premiere Pro, Sketch and InVision making me a great asset to any team.

I love typography and color (especially hues between mint and sapphire). I have a bit of an addiction to National Parks, Oreos, and singing Disney songs enthusiastically in the car.

I’m currently available for both full-time and freelance work in the KC area, remotely and open to relocating.

Thanks for visiting my site and be sure to say hello! I’d love to hear from you.
